The Process

Three steps.
Zero complexity.

No new software to learn. No disruption to your workflow. Northview AI works inside your existing email — exactly where your team already operates.

01

Email Arrives

A tenant, landlord or contractor emails your agency. Nothing changes on their end.

02

Brief Generated

Northview AI reads the email and instantly creates a structured brief — summary, urgency, actions and a suggested reply — ready as a draft in your inbox.

03

Staff Send

Your team open the draft, personalise if needed, and send. No mental load. No wasted time.

Common Questions

Everything you
need to know.

Does it send emails automatically? +
No — Northview AI never sends emails on your behalf. It creates a draft and your staff review, personalise if needed, and send. You remain in full control of every response.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. Your emails are processed solely for the purpose of generating briefs. Data is never shared, resold, or used for external training. Your inbox remains your own.
What email providers does it work with? +
Northview AI is designed to work with the most widely used business email providers. This is confirmed during your setup call — we'll make sure it works with your existing setup before anything goes live.
How long does setup take? +
Most agencies are fully live within 48 hours of their setup call. There's no technical burden on your side — we handle everything and hand it over ready to run.
How many email addresses are included? +
The monthly retainer covers your entire office — all staff email addresses connected to your business are included under one price. No per-user charges.
Can I cancel? +
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel with 30 days notice. We're confident you won't want to once it's running.
